About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow hosted by David Parkes and Yiling Chen at the Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. I am interested in algorithmic problems with economic motivation.

I received my PhD from Tel Aviv University where I was advised by Michal Feldman and Amos Fiat.

Check out my wife's page, Talya Eden. She does awesome things in the field of sampling random thingamajigs.
